Tennis Australia

Datawiresport delivers data including the build, host, maintainance and service; all in-house. Very competitive in a growing market. This project was for their client Tennis Australia and included a player portal, a team captains portal and a matching app. Competitions created in the team captian portal are directly fed into the public portall for all (logged in) players to see.

Flinqer

Built with Foundation, jQuery, Php, sass.
Responsible for design, icons and frontend

Flinqer is a network of companies optimizing the use of available cash. Flinqer matches required discounts by buyers to pay an(y) outstanding invoice immediately with offered discounts by suppliers to get paid immediately – at beneficiary terms for all. Thus becoming less dependent on banks but making cash flow where and when it is needed most at the lowest of costs.
